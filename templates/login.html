About this project

Thanks for being interested in my third year project.

The aim of the project is to enhance travel experience by sharing the travel routes with your friends and planning it based on experiences of friends.

After logging in to the application, you will be able to import your photos, statuses and check-ins from Facebook. Based on their location information, a route will be generated and you can click on the markers to see the photos taken at that place, as well as statuses and check-ins.

You can also see the routes of your friends, and a heatmap showing which places are popular among your friends.

Evaluation of the project

The evaluation of the project consists of two parts. First, please fill the questionnaire one about how you share your travel experiences and make your travel plans. Second, take part in the trial, where you will be using the application and other existing applications and tell me the experience of using them by filling out questionnaire two. It will take 10 mins to fill questionnaire one and 15 mins to do the trial.
